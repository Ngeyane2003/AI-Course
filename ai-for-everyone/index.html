AI for Everyone

This course introduces artificial intelligence concepts for beginners. You'll learn how AI impacts daily life, business, and future opportunities.

Module 1: What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence is the science of making machines think and learn like humans. AI systems analyze data, recognize patterns, and make decisions with minimal human input.

Key takeaway: AI powers the apps, websites, and devices you use every day — even if you don't notice it.

Module 2: Where is AI Used?

  • Netflix recommendations: AI suggests movies and shows based on your viewing habits.
  • Spam filtering: Email systems use AI to block unwanted messages automatically.
  • Car safety alerts: AI sensors warn drivers and prevent collisions.
  • Voice assistants: Devices like Siri and Alexa understand and respond to commands.

Module 3: AI in Business & Life

  • Faster disease detection: AI scans medical data to help doctors detect illness earlier.
  • Self-driving cars: Smart systems navigate roads and avoid hazards.
  • Chatbots & predictions: Businesses use AI to answer questions instantly and forecast customer needs.
  • Fraud detection: AI spots unusual activity to protect accounts and payments.

Conclusion

AI is already shaping how we live and work. Learning how it functions is the first step to staying ahead in an AI-powered world.
